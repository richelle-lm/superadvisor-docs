# Platform Overview 

SuperAdvisor is a comprehensive Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and practice management platform built specifically for wealth management professionals. Unlike generic CRMs that simply track names on a list, SuperAdvisor handles the unique complexities of wealth management.

It allows you to manage entire Households, tracking the intricate web of family members, legal entities, and financial assets—from insurance policies to investment portfolios—that make up a client's total wealth.

## Key Capabilities

* **Relationship Management:** Establish a "Golden Record" of truth for every client and prospect to ensure compliance and deliver hyper-personalized service.
* **Pipeline Tracking:** Forecast revenue, track deal stages, and manage business growth using the robust Opportunities module.
* **Operational Automation:** Standardize complex processes like onboarding and client transitions with the Workflows engine, ensuring no task falls through the cracks.
* **Unified Communication:** Sync your Email and Calendar directly to the platform to keep a centralized history of all client interactions.
* **Financial Aggregation:** Track assets and liabilities in the Financial Records database to power holistic, data-driven financial advice.

Ultimately, SuperAdvisor empowers your team to scale by reducing administrative burdens and providing the insights needed to foster deeper, more profitable client relationships.
